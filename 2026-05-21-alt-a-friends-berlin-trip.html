M3 · The closing ritual

A wrap-up that actually wraps.

Net transfers shown, executed, done — in 60 seconds. The group walks away even. And the next trip starts from "Group still here," not from "who still owes me?"

The wedge · trip-shaped, not transaction-shaped

Who builds for the whole trip — not just the bill?

Tikkie sends payment requests one at a time — a great verb, no narrative arc. Splitwise tracks but doesn't move money. Bunq Groups holds money but has no concept of a "trip" — no start, no end, no settlement ritual. The trip is a unit Rabobank can own — beginning, middle, and the Sunday after.

First 6 months · tangible

Four milestones. One trip-shaped MVP.

Month 1–2

Discovery

Interview 20 friend groups about their last shared trip — what fell apart, what felt good.

Month 3

First MVP

Trip creation + live ledger + one-tap settle. Tikkie import bridge for users mid-flight.

Month 4–5

100-group pilot

Real trips by real Dutch friend groups. Instrument every settlement.

Month 6

First metric report

% of trips that settle within 7 days of return. Plus: trip-pool retention into a second trip.
