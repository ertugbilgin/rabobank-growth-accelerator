The wedge · the silent Kinderrekening

Who actually teaches an 11-year-old?

ING, ABN, Rabobank all show a balance. Bunq Junior adds a card. Revolut <18 adds spending limits. Nobody coaches the trade-off. Nobody turns the first saved euro into a lesson — and the second one into a habit. That's the wedge: the 8–14 window where attitudes form, and where no Dutch bank is present.
