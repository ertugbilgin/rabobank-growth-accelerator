The wedge · 'we' as a customer

Who builds for two people, one life?

Most banking apps treat every account as an individual. Joint accounts exist — but joint goals, joint dashboards, joint mortgage prep do not. Bunq lets couples share a card. Revolut lets couples share a wallet. Neither holds a four-year shared goal. The 'we' customer is invisible to fintech — and visible to Rabobank, where joint mortgages already are core business.
